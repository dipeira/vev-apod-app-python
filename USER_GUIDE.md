# User Guide

This guide is for employees who want to download their salary certificate (Βεβαίωση Αποδοχών).

---

## What You Need

To download your certificate you need three pieces of information:

1. **Φορολογικό Έτος** (Fiscal Year) — the year of the certificate you want
2. **ΑΦΜ** — your 9-digit tax registration number (Αριθμός Φορολογικού Μητρώου)
3. **ΑΜΚΑ** — your 11-digit social security number (Αριθμός Μητρώου Κοινωνικής Ασφάλισης)

---

## How to Download Your Certificate

1. Open the application in your browser (your administrator will provide the URL).

2. The home page shows the **Αναζήτηση Βεβαίωσης** (Certificate Search) form:

   ![Search form with Fiscal Year, AFM, and AMKA fields]

3. Select the **Φορολογικό Έτος** (year) from the dropdown.

4. Enter your **ΑΦΜ** in the AFM field (8–9 digits).

5. Enter your **ΑΜΚΑ** in the AMKA field (11 digits).

6. Click **Λήψη Βεβαίωσης** (Download Certificate).

7. If your details are found, the PDF certificate downloads automatically to your computer.

---

## If No Results Are Found

The page will show:
> "Δεν βρέθηκαν στοιχεία για τα δεδομένα που εισάγατε."

This means no certificate was found for the combination of year, AFM, and AMKA you entered. Check that:
- You selected the correct fiscal year
- You entered your AFM correctly (9 digits)
- You entered your AMKA correctly (11 digits)
- Leading zeros are not required — the system strips them automatically

If you are certain the details are correct, contact your administrator.

---

## If No Years Are Available

The form will show:
> "Δεν υπάρχουν διαθέσιμα δεδομένα αυτή τη στιγμή."

This means no payroll data has been uploaded and processed yet. Contact your administrator.

---

## Privacy

Your AFM and AMKA are sent to the server only to look up your certificate. They are not stored or logged anywhere.

---

## Supported Browsers

Any modern browser works: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari.
